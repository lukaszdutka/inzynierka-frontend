How to use?


Welcome in my Generator! Here you can learn about how to use it properly.

Basics - just click it!

This orange-like rectangle with monster name in it is called Tag. If you want to change them, just click them!



You can also click Tag with right mouse button to reveal menu with more options!

Constant Tags

There are special kind of tags called Constant Tags. Their superpower is that if you change one of them - every other changes too. You can guess what Tags are linked by the same color of border.


Nested Tags

It is also common for Tags to be nested inside other Tags. You can click Parent-Tag to reroll it, along with all Children-Tags!


Aaaaand that's it! Simple, right? There are some more options and even more ideas to develop, but for now... Just enjoy the Generator :)
